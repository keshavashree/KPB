About FoodShare

Bridging the gap between food waste and hunger

Our Mission

FoodShare is dedicated to reducing food waste while fighting hunger in our communities. We provide a seamless platform for restaurants, hotels, and food businesses to donate their surplus food to verified charities and organizations serving those in need.

By connecting donors with receivers, we ensure that good food doesn't go to waste while helping to feed vulnerable populations across our city.

Sustainability Community Innovation

How FoodShare Works

A simple three-step process to reduce food waste

1. Donor Post

Donors post surplus food details including quantity, type, and pickup time through our secure platform.

2. Instant Alert

Receivers receive instant notifications about available food donations matching their criteria and location.

3. Quick Pickup

Receivers coordinate pickup and efficiently distribute food to those who need it most in the community.
